‎ Venus Fly Traps

‎‎‎ and how to care for them



If you just got a venus fly trap
but dont know how to care for
it then you are in the right place

to keep your venus fly traps happy you need to

  • Give it lots of sunlight, if you dont then it cannot grow
  • Place it in a shallow water tray, if you give
    it too much water then you risk over watering, which can kill it
  • Only feed it live insects, if you feed it dead
    bugs then it will not stimulate the trigger hairs and it wont eat it

If you follow these steps correctly then you
and your venus fly trap will be happy!
